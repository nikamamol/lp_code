        The Real Estate Challenge Today

        Real estate businesses are investing significantly in lead generation – Yet the journey from lead to site visit often remains unstructured.

        Common challenges include

        • Leads generated across platforms, but no unified tracking.
        • Limited visibility into lead quality and intent.
        • Delays in response leading to drop-offs.
        • Heavy reliance on manual follow-ups.
        • Difficulty in identifying which campaigns deliver real outcomes.

        As a result, teams struggle to create a consistent and measurable conversion journey.

        Bringing Together Ads, Conversations & Insights

        • Support sales teams with better-qualified prospects

        Consistent Lead Nurturing

        • Continuous engagement across the decision journey
        • Reduced dependency on manual processes

        • Enable more informed campaign decisions over time

              Frequently Asked Questions 

              Who is this seminar designed for?

              This seminar is specifically curated for real estate developers, builders, promoters, sales leaders, marketing teams and channel partners looking to improve lead conversion and customer engagement.

              Is there any cost to attend the seminar?

              No, the seminar is completely free to attend. However, pre-registration is mandatory as seats are limited.

              How can I register for the seminar?

              You can register by filling out the registration form on the event page. Once registered, we will review your profile before sending confirmation email with event details.

              What key topics will be covered in the seminar?

              The session will focus on connecting Meta Ads with real-time conversations, structuring lead qualification, enabling automated follow-ups and understanding campaign performance through analytics.

              Will this seminar include practical use cases or demos?

              Yes, the seminar will include real-world use cases and platform walkthroughs to help you understand how these solutions can be implemented in your business.

              How will this seminar help improve my sales process?

              You will learn how to create a more structured sales journey—from lead generation to booking site visits – by connecting ads, conversations, and analytics into a unified flow.

              Will there be networking opportunities?

              Yes, there will be a dedicated networking session followed by lunch, allowing you to connect with industry peers, experts, and the Pinnacle team.

              What kind of support does Pinnacle provide post-seminar?

              Pinnacle provides end-to-end support, including platform onboarding, training session, ongoing assistance by CSM to help you implement and scale your engagement strategies.

              Do I need any technical knowledge to attend?

              No prior technical expertise is required. The seminar is designed in a simple and practical manner, suitable for both business and technical audiences.

              Will I get access to the platform or solutions discussed?

              Yes, interested participants can explore demos and discuss implementation options with the Pinnacle team during or after the seminar.
